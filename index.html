Strategic Takeaways

Business actions suggested by the dataset patterns.

Protect high-potential SKUs

Computers leads sales potential despite having the fewest products, so inventory and campaign support should focus on its strongest items.

Use Accessories for breadth

Accessories has the largest assortment and best average rating, making it a strong category for bundles and cross-sell offers.

Turn review leaders into anchors

USB-C Hub Model-10, External SSD Model-20, and Smart Watch Model-72 have standout review volume and can anchor promotional placements.

Separate premium from value

The wide price range suggests clear premium and value segments; pricing should be evaluated by rating and review momentum, not price alone.
